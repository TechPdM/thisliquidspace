About

thisLiquidSpace is a small corner of the internet where ideas about digital space, interface design, and the texture of information can exist without needing to justify themselves commercially.

The name comes from a simple observation: nothing online stays still. Every page you visit is assembled on the fly, shaped by your device, your connection, your history. The solid-looking surfaces are all liquid underneath. It seemed worth naming that.

If you want to talk, the best way is to find me where the conversations are already happening. I move through the same liquid space you do.
